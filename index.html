About The Game

LUCKNOW GTA ek realistic open-world mobile game hai jisme player Lucknow-inspired city ko explore karta hai. Game me busy streets, markets, modern areas, vehicles, businesses aur realistic NPC world hoga. Player ek normal insaan ke roop me shuru karega aur apni mehnat, decisions aur skills se shehar me apni pehchan banayega.

Main Story

Chapter 1: A New Beginning

Arjun Malhotra naye sapno ke saath shehar me aata hai. Uski mulaqat Kabir, Riya aur Ananya se hoti hai.


Chapter 2: City Life

Player streets explore karega, shops visit karega, vehicles buy karega aur NPCs ke saath interact karega.


Chapter 3: Building Your Future

Player house kharid sakta hai, vehicles collect kar sakta hai aur business start kar sakta hai.


Chapter 4: Challenges & Missions

City Delivery, Street Race, Rescue Mission aur Mystery Missions.
